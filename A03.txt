Synopsis of PULAU :
"A vacation among friends turns into a horrifying nightmare after they lose a bet and are forced to spend a night on a deserted island. When they stumble upon a mysterious abandoned village there, they accidentally break an old spell placed to restrain an antagonising spirit trapped on the island. One by one, they suffer the gripping and gruesome consequences of releasing an evil creature that needs human blood to stay alive. The only way out is in the hands of a girl who desperately needs to use her supernatural gift to untangle an unsettling history connected to a tainted cross-cultural love story."
